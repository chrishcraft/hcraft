A beautiful new modern farmhouse design for [Align West Homes](https://www.alignwesthomes.com/) coming soon to The Commonage at Predator Ridge!

With design cues from one of their current models, this new home has a spacious main floor with vaulted ceilings in the living area and master suite. The walkout basement is home to the remaining bedrooms and  allows for a great entertainment area. Plenty of outdoor space is available on the large main floor deck with covered patio below. 

Extra luxuries including a small third bay golf cart garage and optional space for a golf simulator! 

This model is starting construction soon at [Predator Ridge](https://www.predatorridge.com/) Contact us for more information!
